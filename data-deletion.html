Data Deletion Instructions

If you have authorized the Learn With Mr. Oddlaw Facebook or Instagram App and wish to delete your data, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings → Apps and Websites.
  2. Find the app named Learn With Mr. Oddlaw.
  3. Select Remove to revoke permissions and delete associated data.
  4. Optionally, send a data deletion request via email to contact@learnwithoddlaw.com with your account ID or username for confirmation.

All user tokens and permissions are automatically deleted when the app is removed from your Facebook or Instagram account.
