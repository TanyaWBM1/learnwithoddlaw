Q 5: How can I win a case without hiring a lawyer?

A: You win by using written procedure — motions, rules, and evidence — to move the judge on law not emotion.


Q 6: What are the “3 Easy Steps to Justice”?

A: Allege the Facts, Prove the Facts, and Move the Judge — the foundation of every case lawyers argue.


Q 7: Why do people lose in court even when they’re right?

Q 8: What is a motion and why is it important?

A: A motion is a written request asking the judge to act. Learning to write and argue motions lets you control the record.

A: Yes. Knowledge of procedure forces fairness — even from judges — because it puts the law on your side.
